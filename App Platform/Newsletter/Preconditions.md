# Newsletter Preconditions

Before working on [[Strategy]], [[Content Calendar]], or
[[Welcome Sequence]], fill this out. A newsletter without answers to
these questions is a dormant list waiting to happen, and a dormant
list is worse than no list at all.

If any of the four answers is "I don't know yet," the honest next
step is to stop newsletter work and go answer that question.

## 1. Who is this list for?

_(Specific person, not a segment. Same rigor as
[[Positioning]] — "indie devs" is not an answer.)_

## 2. What are they reading right now instead?

_(Name the three newsletters, forums, or feeds they already read.
If you cannot, you do not know the audience well enough yet.)_

## 3. What is my non-substitutable point of view?

_(What can I say that those three sources cannot or will not? If the
answer is "the same thing but friendlier," there is no reason for
this list to exist.)_

## 4. What will I eventually sell to this list, and when?

_(Not "we will figure out monetization later." If there is no
product path, the list is a hobby — which is fine, but call it that
and stop calling it a platform.)_

## Green light

This list is ready to plan when all four answers above are concrete
and I can say them out loud without hedging. Until then, newsletter
work is premature.
