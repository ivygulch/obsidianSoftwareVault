# {{title}}

> **status** values: `current` (active engagement) · `past` (completed) · `archive` (lower-signal work kept for completeness).
>
> **employment** values: `employee` (W-2 / FTE) · `contractor` (1099 / direct) · `subcontractor` (through another consulting firm) · `independent` (direct-to-client solo).
>
> **brand** is the product or brand within the parent company if distinct (e.g., "ASDA" or "Sam's Club" for Walmart engagements, "Sharpen" for McGraw-Hill). Leave empty if the engagement was for the parent brand itself.
>
> **industry**, **tech**, **practices** use ATS-canonical casing (e.g., "E-Commerce", "Swift", "Technical Leadership") — spell them the way a job posting would.
>
> **themes** and **deliverables** use internal kebab-case tags (e.g., `architecture`, `testing`, `checkout-platform`) for Dataview grouping across engagements. Not for ATS output.
>
> **scale** values: free-form (e.g., "solo", "small team 1-3", "large team ~120 iOS engineers", "~2M DAU", "500K+ DAU").
