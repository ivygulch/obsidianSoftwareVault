Speechify sits at the intersection of two things I care about. The first is personal: I've spent years as a writer and I value literacy as much as anything I do — I encourage the young people in my life to learn to love reading however they can, and a product whose whole purpose is to remove reading as a barrier, for people with dyslexia, low vision, ADD, or a second language, is one I'd be proud to build. The second is professional: I taught accessibility across Walmart's iOS engineering organization, I've spent a decade shipping iOS at scale, and I'm fluent with the modern Swift and AI tooling Speechify is built on. A high-traffic iOS product at the frontier of AI and audio, with a mission I already believe in, is exactly where I want to put my work.
