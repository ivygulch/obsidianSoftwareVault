# Earlier Career Summary

## Narrative

### Earlier Career — 1980 to 2013

Independent Java / J2EE architect and developer via Sunetos Inc, 1998–2012, across defense, healthcare, mining/manufacturing, higher education, and commercial sectors — MetalsQMS quality-management system for Stillwater Mining (6-year engagement, platinum/palladium, IBM DB2 and JD Edwards ERP integration); distributed logistics tooling for the USAF (WRM-CA, UTC-DT, CBP) and DARPA (ALA, distributed agent research) via Synergy/ICFI; humanitarian aid and disaster relief planning prototype for the DoD via ICF; and multi-industry civilian J2EE work including Navy real-time systems, enterprise workflow, and handheld data sync (J2ME / WinCE).

Prior: Synergy Inc (1996–1998), System Analyst and Programmer on USAF Armstrong Labs (later AFRL) logistics research. Rotman & Sjoquist consulting partnership (1988–1996), serving 12+ colleges and universities concurrent with Cedarville employment. Cedarville College (1982–1996) — software developer, Assistant Director of Computer Services, adjunct instructor (teaching continued 1982–2006). Custom software development for small businesses (1980–1988) during college and high school.
