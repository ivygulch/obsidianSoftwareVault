# CLEAR - Senior iOS Subcontract 2026-present

## Context

Senior iOS subcontract at CLEAR, the biometric identity verification company. CLEAR's primary market is airline travelers: it operates under a legal partnership with the U.S. Transportation Security Administration (TSA), and its flagship iOS app synchronizes users' personal calendars to detect upcoming airline flights and surfaces flight details through a backend FlightAware integration. CLEAR is positioning to expand beyond travel into what it frames as a larger identity-verification market. Engaged to enhance the user experience of the flagship iOS app, evaluate the iOS codebase directly alongside agentic-tool-assisted analysis, recommend architectural and technical direction, and mentor a six-engineer iOS team on incorporating agentic tooling (Claude Code, Codex) into day-to-day development. Paperwork finalized 2026-02-06; engagement started shortly after.

## Role

Senior iOS subcontractor whose primary focus is enhancing the user experience of CLEAR's flagship iOS app, alongside architectural evaluation and mentorship — UX-focused feature and flow work, direct codebase review, architectural recommendations, and hands-on mentorship for the iOS team on agentic development workflows across the full lifecycle. Also leading the modernization of the production app to Swift 6.2+ with structured concurrency.
