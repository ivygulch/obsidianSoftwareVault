# Synergy - USAF Armstrong Labs 1996-1998

## Context

Two-year full-time employment at Synergy Inc (later rebranded ICFI following acquisition) as System Analyst and Programmer. Developed software on contract to the Logistics Research division of the USAF's Armstrong Labs (absorbed into AFRL in 1997). Transitioned from Cedarville College at Rotman & Sjoquist dissolution; transitioned out of W-2 employment in 1998 to form Sunetos Inc and continue the same work lineage as an independent contractor.

## Role

System Analyst and Programmer on Logistics Research contracts — system analysis, architectural design, and development of distributed logistics tools for the Air Force.

## Achievements

- Designed and began development of UTC-DT (Unit Type Code Development and Tailoring) — a peer-to-peer distributed system automating generation of equipment requirements, with a custom socket protocol for efficient multi-user data updates. (UTC-DT development spanned 1996-1999, beginning as employee and continuing as independent contractor.) #architecture #distributed-systems #peer-to-peer #defense
- Worked on USAF Armstrong Labs / Logistics Research contracts as a system analyst and programmer. #defense #research #systems-analysis
