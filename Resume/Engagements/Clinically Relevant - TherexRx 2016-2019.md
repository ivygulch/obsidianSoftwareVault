# Clinically Relevant - TherexRx 2016-2019

## Context

Independent engagement on TherexRx, an iOS app for physical therapists to develop and prescribe individualized treatment and exercise plans for rehabilitation patients. Early work began as the "LBP" (Low Back Pain) iPhone app proposal in October 2016 and evolved into TherexRx, a broader therapy-prescription platform. Backend API development was shared with a separate subcontractor.

## Role

Lead iOS developer owning the TherexRx iOS app end-to-end — architecture, feature development, data synchronization with a distributed backend, App Store beta release, and ongoing support through wrap-up in early 2019.

## Achievements

- Developed TherexRx iOS app enabling physical therapists to create and prescribe individualized treatment and rehabilitation plans. #healthcare #ios #feature-delivery
- Architected data synchronization between iOS client, backend API, and a distributed database backed by Realm. #data-sync #backend-integration #realm
- Shipped TherexRx to App Store beta with active external beta testers by late 2018. #beta-testing #release #app-store

## Tech Stack

Swift, iOS SDK, Realm (distributed database), RESTful backend APIs, Core Data.
