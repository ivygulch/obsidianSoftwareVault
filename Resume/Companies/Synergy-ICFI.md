# Synergy Inc (later ICFI)

## About

Research and consulting firm operating in the defense and aerospace sector. Employed me as System Analyst and Programmer 1996-1998 on contracts to the Logistics Research division of the USAF's Armstrong Labs (later absorbed into AFRL). Synergy was subsequently acquired and rebranded as ICFI, which continued as a contracting entity for follow-on USAF and DARPA work through Sunetos into the mid-2000s.
