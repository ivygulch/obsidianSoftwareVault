# Shelved Ideas MOC

Ideas that were killed, parked, or superseded. This is not a graveyard —
it is research. Shelved ideas show what I am drawn to and what I keep
rejecting, and they are the first place to look when a new seed feels
familiar.

## Conventions

- An idea is moved here (not deleted) when it hits kill criteria or
  when I decide not to pursue it.
- Set `status: shelved` in the frontmatter and add a `shelved_on:` date.
- Add a **Why Shelved** section to the top of the note before moving
  it. One paragraph. Be specific about which kill criterion fired or
  what changed.
- Do not edit the original concept sections after shelving — the value
  is in the frozen record, not a rewrite.
