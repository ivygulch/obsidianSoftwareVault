# Collectibles

Apps for managing collections and badge/stamp/patch/pin systems — physical, digital, or bridged. Cross-cutting: applies to parks and trails, books, coffees, concerts, and anything else worth tracking.

## Focus Areas
- **Digital collectibles** — virtual stamps/badges/patches earned via geofencing, photo proof, or manual entry
- **Physical-to-digital bridge** — scan real passport stamps, patches, and pins into a managed collection
- **Hobby collections** — books read, coffees tried, concerts attended, restaurants visited, trails walked
- **Display & sharing** — showcase, print, export, gift
