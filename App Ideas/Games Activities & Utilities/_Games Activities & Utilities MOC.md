# Games, Activities & Utilities

Games, activities, and small utility apps themed to any of the other areas. This is where ideas land when the *format* (game, utility, micro-tool) matters more than the domain.

## Focus Areas
- **Writing games** — daily prompt challenges, constrained-writing puzzles, story dice
- **Reminiscence games** — photo-based recall, life-story prompts as games (ties to [[../Story Capture/_Story Capture MOC|Story Capture]])
- **Outdoor games** — scavenger hunts, trail bingo, family road-trip games (ties to [[../Wilderness & Outdoor/_Wilderness & Outdoor MOC|Wilderness & Outdoor]])
- **Themed utilities** — timers, trackers, calculators, reference tools scoped to a specific activity or hobby
