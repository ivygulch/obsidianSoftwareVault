# Story Capture

Apps for helping people record memories, life stories, and lessons to pass on. Includes family and intergenerational tools.

## Focus Areas
- **Personal memoir** — guided life-story prompts, timeline builders, weaving photo/audio/video
- **LLM-assisted interviews** — conversational capture that branches on what's already been said; local or cloud LLM; self-guided or with a helper (family member, caregiver)
- **Heirloom & ethical wills** — letters for later, lessons for grandchildren, values and belief capture
- **Oral history** — community, veteran, diaspora, and genealogy-linked narratives
- **Object stories** — provenance and memory attached to photos of meaningful objects
- **Family & intergenerational** — shared family archives, grandparent↔grandchild prompts, recipe stories, reminiscence
