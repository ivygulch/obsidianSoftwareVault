# App Ideas

Brainstorming space for app concepts, organized by area. No development work — this is the thinking space. Ideas that graduate to active work move to [[_Projects MOC|Projects]].

## Areas
- [[Writing/_Writing MOC|Writing]] — apps for authors and readers
- [[Story Capture/_Story Capture MOC|Story Capture]] — memoirs, interview-assisted capture, family & intergenerational
- [[Wilderness & Outdoor/_Wilderness & Outdoor MOC|Wilderness & Outdoor]] — trip capture, planning, skills, citizen science
- [[Collectibles/_Collectibles MOC|Collectibles]] — badges, stamps, patches, pins; physical-to-digital collections
- [[Games Activities & Utilities/_Games Activities & Utilities MOC|Games, Activities & Utilities]] — themed to any other area
- [[Miscellaneous/_Miscellaneous MOC|Miscellaneous]] — adjacent ideas and anything not yet categorized

## Conventions
- One page per concept; use the [[App Idea]] template.
- Set `status:` frontmatter to `seed`, `exploring`, `shelved`, or `promoted`.
- Promoted ideas move to [[_Projects MOC|Projects/]] with their own project folder.

## All Ideas (Dataview)

FROM "App Ideas"
