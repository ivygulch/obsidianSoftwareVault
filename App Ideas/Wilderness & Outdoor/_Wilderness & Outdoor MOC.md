# Wilderness & Outdoor

Apps for people who spend time outside — capturing trips, planning adventures, building skills, and connecting outdoor activity to memory and learning.

## Focus Areas
- **Trip & memory capture** — structured logs for paddling, hiking, climbing, fishing, etc., with conditions, GPS, photos
- **Planning** — routes, gear lists by trip type, weather/tide/flow integration, permit reminders
- **Skill-building** — knots, navigation, naturalist ID, survival drills; gamified micro-lessons
- **Citizen science** — bird/plant/water-quality logging tied to trips
- **Outdoor gamification** — scavenger hunts, trail bingo, family road-trip games
